What We Do

Food Bank For New York City is a non-profit social services organization and the major hunger-relief organization working against hunger in the five boroughs. Its aim is to organize food, information and support for needy citizens of New York City.

How to Volunteer

Covid-19 Update: All Volunteer Services Temporarily Suspended

We appreciate all the volunteer support we have recieved. However, due to COVID-19 we have shifted to paid temporary workers and will not be accepting volunteers at this time.
